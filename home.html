About Apple

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California, that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. Apple is the world's largest technology company by revenue and, as of January 2021, the world's most valuable company.

UNIVERSITY STUDENTS

University students on the Apple website can access exclusive discounts on products, explore student-friendly apps, and benefit from educational resources designed to enhance their learning and productivity.

iPhone 15 Pro

The iPhone 15 Pro features a sleek design, advanced A17 Bionic chip, improved camera system with enhanced low-light performance, ProMotion display, longer battery life, and new titanium frame for added durability.

iPhone 15

The iPhone 15 features an upgraded camera system, enhanced performance with the latest A-series chip, improved battery life, a sleek design, and advanced software capabilities, making it a top choice for tech enthusiasts.

iPad Pro

The iPad Pro is a powerful tablet by Apple, featuring a high-resolution display, advanced performance with the M1 or M2 chip, and compatibility with accessories like the Apple Pencil and Magic Keyboard.

Trade In

Apple's trade-in program allows customers to exchange their old devices for credit towards new purchases. It's an eco-friendly initiative encouraging recycling and upgrading to the latest Apple products.
